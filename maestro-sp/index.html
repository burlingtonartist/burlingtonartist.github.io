Maestro Specialty Corporation (MSC)

A licensed NY State reinsurance intermediary.

Our company is an insurance program placement broker, and an insurance program design consultant. MSC has identified a specialized insurance industry market opportunity that is currently being under-served by its large competitors. We bring experience and know-how to the table, assitiing you in construction of the best programs possible.

In partnership with carefully chosen successful Managing General Agencies (MGAs)*, or by working with talented underwriting entrepreneurs who wish to create their own MGA, MSC will assist the MGA in finding new program insurance carrier partners. MSC will also assist in the placement of any reinsurance on the specific program as required.

Next

The Primary MGA Conundrum:

An MGA, typically owned by one principal, continually faces operational disruptions from turnover in his program insurance company partners given the short term cancellation provisions (usually 90 to 180 days) found in most MGA Agreements.Cancellation rationale can include the cyclical nature of profitability in the insurance industry, plus fundamental shifts in an insurance company's: 1) underwriting appetite; 2) ownership or statutory surplus; 3) philosophy on programs; and/or 4) program oversight personnel. This threat of cancellation, plus restrictive operating terms in his existing MGA Agreement, often requires the MGA principal to spend a disproportionate amount of time searching for new partners.
