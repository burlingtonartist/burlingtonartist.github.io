Successful Program Placements

Explore some of the different types of programs Maestro has successfully placed

Residential and Commercial Contractors Program

Problem:

MGA approached Maestro in need of assistance placing a residential and commercial contractor including construction defect states.

Solution:

Maestro secured an AM Best’s A- rated carrier looking to expand in the construction space.

Homeowners Program

Problem:

MGA approached Maestro in search for an AM Best rated A+ carrier to strengthen their homeowners portfolio.

Solution:

Maestro secured an AM Best’s A++ rated carrier for the MGA’s coastal writings in the southeast.

Taxi/Limo Program

Problem:

MGA approached Maestro to carve taxi/limo business out of their existing portfolio to create a new program.

Solution:

Maestro secured an AM Best’s A rated carrier that had no channel conflicts.

Nightclub Program

Problem:

MGA purchased the renewal rights to an insurance company’s book of business and needed to find a new carrier within a very short timeframe.

Solution:

Maestro secured an AM Best’s A+ rated carrier within 60 days.

Roofing Contractors Program

Problem:

Wholesaler approached Maestro about finding a carrier that would allow them to underwrite roofing contractors.

Solution:

Maestro secured an AM Best’s A+ rated carrier willing to partner with the wholesaler once rates and underwriting guidelines were predetermined.

Surety Program

Problem:

MGA approached Maestro to find a carrier that would allow them to underwrite small contractors bonds in California on the carrier’s behalf.

Solution:

Maestro secured a regional AM Best’s A rated carrier that was looking to expand into California.

Trucking Program

Problem:

MGA wished to bifurcate their existing trucking program with another quality program insurance carrier.

Solution:

Maestro secured an AM Best’s A rated carrier to match the existing terms and conditions of the MGA’s current carrier which resulted in a 50/50 split of the program.
