Our Hotel

From I-240 either East or West, take the Germantown/Poplar exit 15A East (Highway 72). Once on Poplar Ave, prepare for a left turn. Turn left at first traffic light onto Ridgeway Loop Road. Stay in the left lane until you turn left at second traffic light onto Ridge Lake Blvd. The hotel will be straight ahead on the left side.
